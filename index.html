Structure

Amenities

Fully-furnished 5-bedroom home with mountain vistas and multiple common areas including entertainment. Large pool area with spa, 10 minutes from the beach and mountain trails, 5 minutes from the Camarillo Premium Outlets, and walking distance to local 12-Step meetings.

In-house Support & Activities

3 in-house support coaches, Weekly in-house 12-Step meeting, sober social events, and offsite intensive outpatient programs with licensed therapists (transportation provided).

Career and Continuing Education

Mission house strongly promotes employment and education. We will assist you with enrolling in school and or finding employment. We will help you get back into the workforce and on a path to success.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide a sober, safe, and structured environment for addicts and alcoholics making the transition back into everyday life. With the guidance and support of our 3 live-in sober coaches we encourage accountability and clients will learn how to regain a sense of self-worth and maintain a healthy and happy lifestyle.

Mission House’s prime location provides great accessibility to daily 12-step meetings within walking distance and a meeting hall which hosts 3-4 meetings a day that is less than 3 miles away. Mission House is near shopping, dining, entertainment, the beach, and many outdoor activities. Mission House offers off-site IOP and therapy services. Additionally, we host 12-Step meetings and recovery-related events.

Mission House aims to forge strong and healthy individuals and provide a domain in which recovery and self-development can flourish. We sincerely hope that if you find yourself in need of such an environment, that our recovery house can provide you with a stable foundation to walk the road of happy destiny.

About

Mission House is a sober living that aids individuals as they recover from the disease of alcoholism and addiction, as they make their first steps toward a new and healthy lifestyle. Mission House takes pride in being a successful recovery environment providing safety, support, and structure for those recovering from their disease. Mission House is a large and comfortable sober living home in the foothills of Camarillo, surrounded by Malibu, Ventura, and Thousand Oaks. We have a zero-tolerance policy for drug and/or alcohol use and/or possession, and housing is contingent on the client’s obligation to remain sober and adhere to our house rules. Each client is required to actively work a program of recovery with a sponsor. Here at Mission House, we understand the critical role a healthy and structured environment plays in each recovering addict’s life. A structured and supportive environment is critical to the individual in early recovery.
